Titanic Facts

Some interesting facts we pulled together related to the Titanic dataset from Kaggle.

Overall Dataset Analysis

  • Total Passengers: 891
  • Gender Split: 65% of the people on board were male.
  • Survival Rate: 38% of people on board survived
  • Survival Rate (Gender): 19% of men survived, 74% of women survived.
  • Room on door: There was, in fact, room on the door for Jack.

Dataset Age Analysis

  • Youngest passenger: The youngest passenger was 22 weeks old.
  • Oldest Passenger: The oldest passenger in the dataset was 80 years old.
  • Average Age: The average age of a passenger was 30 years old, and the Median age was 28.
  • Mode: The most common ages were babies (less than 1 year old) and 24.
  • The large number of babies on board caused a strange distribution for the dataset.
  • We saw lots of outliers on the upper ends in lower class tickets.
  • One limitation for the dataset, only 80% of passengers had their age listed.

Dataset Ticket Analysis

  • Average Ticket Price: The average ticket price was £32, with a median price of £14. That would be £3,682.59 and £1,611.13 in 2019 pounds!
  • Most Expensive ticket: The most expensive ticket was £512.33. That's £58,921.37 in 2019 pounds!
  • Least Expensive Ticket: £0.00. 15 people boarded for free!
  • Class: 24% of tickets were 1st class, 21% were 2nd class, 55% were 3rd class
  • Survival Rate by Class: 63% of 1st class survived, 47% of 2nd class survived, and 24% of 3rd class survived.
